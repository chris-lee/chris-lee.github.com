Time for a Change

Dec 1st, 2012 | Comments

Two years ago I launched anotherchrislee.com with the goal of writing about my MBA experience and startups. A handful of posts came out of it, but nothing too exciting so today I’m relaunching my blog with a new look and feel (more about that in a sec) and a narrower focus on lessons learned launching my first startup, Meeteor.

We’re over two years in to that adventure and I never could’ve guessed just how much I didn’t know when first starting a company. One of the main reasons for this blog is to document that knowledge for the future and (hopefully) help someone else learn from it.
